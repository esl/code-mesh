Yodit Stanton

The Internet of Things in Practice

We are at the dawn of an age of connected things, Internet of Things (IOT), some predict that there will be 25 billion new connected devices over the new few years.

This talk isn't about the hype but to delve into the hows. We will explore the protocols, the message queues and the eternal problem of standards.

We will hook up some objects to the internet and hopefully give everyone in the room the maker bug.

Talk objectives: It's been a success if everyone leaves the talk excited to hack on some hardware.

Target audience: For the curious.

About Yodit

Yodit is a software developer and founder of opensensors.IO. She spends her time hacking hardware and measuring ALL the things.
