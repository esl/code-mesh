0 – 100 MPH - Launching a New Product at Scale

Dan Macklin

0 – 100 MPH - Launching a New Product at Scale

In this talk Dan will explain why bet365 has adopted erlang as a core development platform and go through the highs and lows of managing change in one of the world's biggest on-line bookmakers.

Talk objectives: to help organisations successfully migrate to innovative and non-mainstream languages

Target audience: anyone who needs to manage radical change to succeed

About Dan

Dan is a hands-on senior technical manager who excels in fast paced environments where expectations are high, deadlines are tight and problems are challenging. Currently he is a Research and Development Manager at Bet365.
